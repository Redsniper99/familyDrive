About us

More Than a Ride —
It’s Peace of Mind.

FamilyDriver is a Sri Lankan-based mobile application built to provide a reliable, safe, and user-friendly driver-on-demand service. Our platform connects users with professional drivers who can operate their personal vehicles — whether for a night out, a medical visit, an airport drop-off, or simply when you’d rather not drive yourself.

We are proud to be locally developed by Metafuse, combining modern technology with a strong focus on trust, safety, and convenience.

At FamilyDriver, we believe safety begins long before the engine starts. Our mission is simple: to make driving more responsible, more reliable, and more human. Born out of real-life needs in busy Colombo streets, FamilyDriver is more than just an app — it’s a movement towards safer mobility, built for the moments when you shouldn’t be behind the wheel, but your car still needs to get you home.

Every trip is powered by professionalism, driven by trust, and made possible through technology crafted with care. Whether you’re booking for yourself, your loved ones, or a guest, FamilyDriver ensures the ride is smooth, secure, and just a tap away. Because sometimes the most responsible decision... is not to drive.
